Indroduction

My name is Wazir Ali Kazim. I was born on April 4, 2007, in Stak Roundu, Gilgit-Baltistan. At the age of seven, I began my educational journey by enrolling in Baltistan Education System, Stak Roundu, where I completed my studies from nursery to matriculation. This twelve-year educational experience played a vital role in building a strong academic foundation. After completing my matriculation, I enrolled as an ICS student at Government Boys Degree College, Skardu, where I successfully completed my FSc. Following this, I gained admission to Karakoram International University in the Computer Science department. The university's quality education system and the guidance of hardworking teachers have provided me with valuable learning opportunities. As part of my learning and practice, I developed my personal website, and my goal is to continue creating beautiful, modern, and professional websites in the future.
